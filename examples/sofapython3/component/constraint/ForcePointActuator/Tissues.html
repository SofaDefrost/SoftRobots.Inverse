ForcePointActuator

Simulation of two colliding soft bodies. We control the position (white sphere) of a point of the underlaying red body by applying a force on the wall.

It uses the QPInverseProblemSolver, ForcePointActuator and PositionEffector components, available in the SoftRobots plugin.




How to

Select the sphere (shift + click left) and move it around. The effector (green square) will follow the sphere (if possible) by applying a force along a given direction.

Further descriptions of the component can be found at: https://project.inria.fr/softrobot/documentation/
